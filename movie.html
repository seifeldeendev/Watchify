The Green Mile

About: Set in a 1935 Southern prison, the film tells the story of a corrections officer who develops a profound relationship with an inmate who possesses mysterious healing powers. As the officer learns more about the inmate's past and abilities, he grapples with questions of justice, redemption, and the nature of miracles.

Starring: Tom Hanks, Michael Clarke Duncan, David Morse

Director: Frank Darabont

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Mystery

Rating: Rated 18+ for violence, language, and some sex-related material
